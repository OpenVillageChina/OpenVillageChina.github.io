Anhui

Anhui (安徽) is a landlocked province of China located in the eastern region of the country. Located across the basins of the Yangtze River and the Huai River, it borders Jiangsu to the east, Zhejiang to the southeast, Jiangxi to the south, Hubei to the southwest, Henan to the northwest, and Shandong for a short section in the north. The provincial capital is Hefei. More from Wikipedia...

Chuzhou

Chuzhou (滁州) is a prefecture-level city in eastern Anhui Province. It borders the provincial capital of Hefei to the southwest, Huainan to the west, Bengbu to the northwest, Chaohu to the south, and the province of Jiangsu to the east. The city has a population of almost 4 million residents. More from Wikipedia
