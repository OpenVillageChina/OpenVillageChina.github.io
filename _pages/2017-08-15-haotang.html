Located in the southern rural area of Xinyang City, Haotang covers 22 square kilometers of fields, mountains and stream. There are 18 villager teams which include 2,385 individuals in Haotang. Haotang is famous for its picturesque views. While the guests enjoy their trip beside traditional buildings and quiet forest path, they can also taste fine local food, like lotus seeds, garden pea jelly and plum juice.

Location: Hao Tang is located on the south of He Nan province and not far from Hu Bei Province.

Tourism

The tourism is the majority of the income,including hotel industries and by-products industries.

Notable attractions include:

  1. Lotus pond
  2. Special dwellings(No.1 Courtyard,No.2 Courtyard and so on)
  3. Public space called An Zhi Xuan(meaning the pavilion on the riverside)
  4. Some characteristic food of farmers(ex:Di Guo Meal---A kind of traditional way of cooking)
  5. Some local pickles(watermelon pickles,peanut pickles,beef pickles,chicken pickles and so on)

Problems faced by the village

Hao Tang Village is facing the problem of tourism and community.For community,the support of local government has been weakening gradually.Hao Tang has been developing in a high speed since 2011 with the strong support government,local authority and village leaders.However,nowadays Hao Tang is a pattern of “rural development” more than the target which government highly concerned. As a result,villagers feel unsafe and they are unwilling to take actions.The key solution is to help the villagers to build the thought and competence of independent development.

The main problem of tourism is homogenization since the pattern can be replicated easily and at low price.At the same time,it faces the problem of the tourists’ source shortage which is considered to be linked with the brand stratages of Hao Tang.To be specific,the tourists are mainly from He Nan Province(Xin Yang,Zhu Ma Dian,Zhen Zhou and nearby areas) who have specific objection and expectation such as peasant meal,fruit picking,family excursion and so on.Their consumption is mainly on catering and its by-product.However almost all of these “expectation” mentioned above can be provided by ordinary Farmhouse near the cities.What makes the Hao Tang maintains its competitiveness are its reputation and environment.Therefore, the differentiation of tourism development, is the key to find path of sustainable development.

Opportunities

Short-term

More and more young people have left cities for their hometown and they are making efforts on the development of Hao Tang.

Medium-term

The Local NGO are trying to build a virtual community so that the resources and information can be exchanged freely among villagers.If the idea can be turned into reality,it will not not only avoid the obstacle of communication,but also open up the minds of villagers.

The Village Leaders and Local NGO expects to plan a stationary bazaar on the villager in order to regulate the current market .

The NGO is preparing to provide some course for the dwellings managers which can help them command the basic knowledge of network marketing.

Long-term

The tourism is likely to get out of the bottleneck and realize the diversity development.

Detailed Information

Climate

Annual rainfall 900-1400mm
Average temperature 15 °C

Infrastructure

Water The drinking water is enough.It is from the small reservoir beside Knit Art Garden built by the Safe Drinking Water Project .Now it is facing the problem of unstable hydraulic pressure and occasional water failure.
Sewage There are 200 water-washed three-septic tank toilets and 150 square artificial wetlands sewage treatment system.
Heating Almost every household has air-condition.
Gas The majority of household uses canned gas while a few use coal and firewood.
Electricity The electricity is accessible to the whole village.However,some household is facing the problem of unstable voltage.The failure of the government’s Electricity Grid Renovation Project is considered as the main reason accounting for it.Besides,the reaction of villagers is useless and now the electricity is being cut off sometimes.
Internet The signal of the whole village is strong and stable.
Phone Almost everyone has phones.
Roads It includes the stone road and hardening road.The road leads to every place and the stone road creates a special atmosphere.Besides,there is a bike path of 33.4km(the cycling competition was hold once)

Nature

Plants The plants in village are not distinctive.There are some old ginkgoes which have history of more than one hundred years.

Economic and Social

Total village income The total income of the village was 20 million yuan in 2013.
Administrative districts It has 18 villagers groups.
Number of families 640
Individuals 2,385
Party members 50
Inequality The development of tourism is unbalanced in village.Hong Xing villager group is the front runner in tourism development.

Politics

Who are the officials?

The Chinese traditional rural autonomous political system can be seen in village.The most important officials are Village Head and Party Secretary.

What system is used to run the village?

Every 15 households elected a villagers on behalf of them (Representatives of Villagers). Villagers' Congress includes Village Leaders (two commissioners:Village Head and Party Secretary), Villager Group Leaders and Representatives of Villagers (Total:42 people). The important affairs are proposed by Party Branch, discussed by two commissioners and reviewed by Party Member Congress. The implementation is based on certain procedure which helps the villagers understand clearly.

Agriculture and Industry

Crops The main crops are rice,tea and Chinese chestnut.
Land surface available for plowing 1972 mu paddy field and 420 mu dry land.
